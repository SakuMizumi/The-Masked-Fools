Sparkle


Oh you want to know more about me? Well I am Sparkle. You can see a portrait of myself on the right of the screen.
Here's a list of what I have done so far:
  • In 2.0, I was introduced in Honkai: Star Rail as a five star character.
  • In the whole Penacony arc, I helped the Stellaron Hunters to ensure Firefly's (or SAM) safety through her three "deaths". And I helped Aventurine and your train buddies to uncover the conspiracy within The Family.
  • In 2.3, on the Radiant Feldspar, I hosted a game: I placed 1000 dolls all over the ship (one of them is a "bomb"). The "bomb" left a magnificent firework show! It was so much fun to see everyone panicking! I had a good laugh...
  • In Honkai Impact 3rd, version 7.8, I entered the quantum computer that is Mars, and I met Vita. I encouraged her to join us.
  • In 7.9, I'll finally be playable in Honkai Impact 3rd! Come see me! I brought some surprises as well...
